Hey! What happened to coxblocks.rocks?

Ah, good question!

In early September of 2020, the current player community decided that they'd prefer to be known by a name that wasn't referential to any specific person, and legos.digital was just too funny to pass up.

Wait, why?

Our old URL was owned by someone we didn't want to talk to about the future of the server. If you still need more info, check the links below.

  • Do By Friday, Episode 190
  • Polygon.com Report, Jun 23, 2020

Oh, that seems like a bad association for a fun little minecraft community to have.

Yeah, we thought so too, so we changed our name and URL, and the server is now entirely run by the same community members who play on the server.

Ok, well, can I still play minecraft?

Of course! You can play at our new URL, "play.legos.digital". The server is running Java 1.16.4.
